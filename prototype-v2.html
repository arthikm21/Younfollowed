How to get your data

Step 1 — Download your Instagram data

⏳

Instagram takes 24–48 hours to prepare your file. Start the request now while you explore.

1
Open Instagram Go to your Profile, then tap the Menu icon (≡) in the top right
2
Find "Download Your Information" Settings & Privacy → Your Activity → Download Your Information
3
Select data to export Choose "Followers and Following" — or select All Data for a full export
4
Choose JSON format This is critical — YOUnfollowed only reads JSON, not HTML
⚠ Select JSON, not HTML
5
Request your download Tap "Create Files" — Instagram will email you when it's ready (24–48h)
6
Download the ZIP file Check your email and tap the download link — you'll get a .zip file
Come back here once downloaded
